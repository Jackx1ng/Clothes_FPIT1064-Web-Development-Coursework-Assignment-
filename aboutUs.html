About us

Welcome to Aquila, a fashion brand founded locally in Malaysia at year 2021 by Brandon Koo, Mincent Lim, and Jack Xing. In Aquila, you can find unique, new, stylish and exquisite clothing designs which are desingned by our own design team which consist of sophisticated fashion designers from Malaysia. From casual wear to formal business wear, we got it all for you. Our goal is to let our fashion designs be seen by everyone from around the globe and build a name for ourselves and furthermore redefine fashion with our own style.
